<p><p>Optical Low Pass Filters (OLPF) are designed to reduce aliasing and moiré effects in imaging systems by allowing low-frequency light to pass while blocking high-frequency details. The market for OLPFs is segmented into three types: Single Layer Filters, which consist of a single coated layer for basic filtering; Two Layer Filters, which offer enhanced performance through an additional layer for improved transmission and reduced distortion; and Multilayer Filters, which provide superior control over light wavelengths, ensuring optimal image quality in advanced optical applications.</p></p>

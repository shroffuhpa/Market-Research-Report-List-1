<p><p>The wired workout headphone market features two primary types: closed-back and open-back models. Closed-back headphones provide isolation by enclosing the ear, minimizing outside noise and enhancing bass response, making them ideal for immersive workout sessions. In contrast, open-back headphones allow sound to flow in and out, creating a more natural listening experience but offering less sound isolation. Both types cater to different user preferences, with closed-backs favored for intense workouts and open-backs preferred for lighter activities or gym environments.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Bullet Train and High-Speed Rail Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Wheel on Rail"</li><li>"Maglev"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bullet train and high-speed rail market can be categorized into two main types: "Wheel on Rail" and "Maglev." The "Wheel on Rail" market uses traditional steel wheels running on steel tracks, providing a cost-effective and efficient means of transportation with speeds typically reaching 300 km/h. In contrast, the "Maglev" market employs magnetic levitation technology, allowing trains to hover above tracks, resulting in minimal friction and enabling higher speeds, often exceeding 600 km/h, along with smoother, quieter rides.</p></p>

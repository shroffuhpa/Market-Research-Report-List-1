<p><p>The polypropylene microsphere market is categorized into two main types: hollow microspheres and solid microspheres. Hollow microspheres are lightweight particles often used in applications requiring low density and improved thermal insulation, such as in coatings and plastics. Solid microspheres, on the other hand, are dense particles utilized for their strength and durability, commonly found in automotive and construction materials. Both types serve diverse industries, enhancing product performance and offering specific functional benefits tailored to various applications.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Multifocal IOL Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Refractive Type</li><li>Diffraction Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multifocal IOL market is primarily categorized into refractive and diffraction types. Refractive multifocal IOLs utilize varied lens curvatures to address different vision zones, allowing users to see clearly at multiple distances. In contrast, diffraction type IOLs employ diffractive optics to create multiple foci, distributing light to enhance vision for both near and far sight. Each type offers unique benefits for cataract surgery patients, aimed at improving overall visual acuity while minimizing dependence on corrective eyewear.</p></p>

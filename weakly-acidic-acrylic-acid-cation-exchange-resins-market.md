<p><p>Weakly acidic acrylic acid cation exchange resins are categorized into Type I and Type II. Type I resins have higher acid dissociation constants, allowing them to effectively treat soft waters and remove specific metal ions. They maintain effectiveness in low pH environments. Type II resins, with lower dissociation constants, are suitable for harsher conditions and are more efficient in a broader pH range, making them ideal for applications requiring robust ion exchange capabilities. Both types serve diverse industrial needs.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Wet Grinding</li><li>Dry Grinding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tungsten electrode grinder market is segmented into two primary types: wet grinding and dry grinding. Wet grinding involves using a coolant to reduce heat and prevent electrode damage, resulting in smoother finishes and extended tool life. This method is preferred for its efficiency and precision. In contrast, dry grinding eliminates coolant, making it simpler and more portable, but it may generate more heat and wear on the electrode. Each method caters to specific user preferences and application requirements.</p></p>

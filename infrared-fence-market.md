<p><strong>The Infrared Fence Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Infrared Fence</li><li>Active Infrared Fence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared fence market primarily includes two types: passive infrared fences and active infrared fences. Passive infrared fences utilize sensors to detect changes in infrared radiation caused by moving objects, alerting security systems when intrusions occur. In contrast, active infrared fences use a series of transmitters and receivers to create an invisible barrier; when an object interrupts the beam, it triggers an alarm. Both types are essential for enhancing perimeter security in various applications, including residential and commercial properties.</p></p>

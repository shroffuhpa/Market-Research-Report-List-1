<p><ul><li>Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis</li><li>Automated Peritoneal Dialysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Peritoneal Dialysis Equipment Market comprises two primary types: Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis (CAPD) and Automated Peritoneal Dialysis (APD). CAPD involves manual fluid exchanges performed by patients throughout the day, allowing flexibility and independence. In contrast, APD employs a machine to automate and optimize fluid exchanges, usually overnight, enhancing convenience and efficiency. Both methods serve patients with kidney failure, offering alternatives to traditional hemodialysis by utilizing the peritoneal membrane for waste removal and fluid regulation.</p></p>

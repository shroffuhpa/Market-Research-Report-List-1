<p><strong>The Electric Arc Furnaces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC-EAF</li><li>DC-EAF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Arc Furnaces (EAF) are crucial in steel production, categorized mainly into AC (Alternating Current) EAF and DC (Direct Current) EAF. AC-EAFs utilize alternating current to melt scrap metal, offering flexibility and lower operational costs, making them popular for recycling operations. In contrast, DC-EAFs employ direct current, providing greater efficiency and a more uniform heat, suited for producing high-quality steel. Both types cater to different market needs, balancing production efficiency, energy consumption, and output quality in the steelmaking process.</p></p>

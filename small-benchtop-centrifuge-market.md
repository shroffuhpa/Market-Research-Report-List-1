<p><strong>The Small Benchtop Centrifuge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ventilated Centrifuge</li><li>Refrigerated Centrifuge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small benchtop centrifuge market includes two primary types: ventilated centrifuges and refrigerated centrifuges. Ventilated centrifuges are designed for routine applications, providing airflow to prevent overheating during centrifugation. They are cost-effective and suitable for basic separation tasks. Refrigerated centrifuges, on the other hand, maintain low temperatures throughout the process, making them ideal for heat-sensitive samples. This type is widely used in research and clinical laboratories where precise temperature control is crucial for sample integrity.</p></p>

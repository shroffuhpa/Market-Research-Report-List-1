<p><strong>The Vascular Embolization Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coiling Devices</li><li>Non-Coiling Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vascular embolization devices market is segmented into coiling and non-coiling devices. Coiling devices involve the use of thin wire coils that are deployed within blood vessels to occlude them, primarily for treating aneurysms or vascular malformations. Non-coiling devices include a variety of tools such as liquid embolic agents, particles, and plugs that block blood flow without using coils. Both device types are essential for minimally invasive procedures aimed at managing vascular conditions effectively.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Hypertension Drug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Calcium Channel Blockers</li><li>Diuretics</li><li>Beta Blockers Vasodilators</li><li>Angiotensin Converting Enzyme Inhibitors</li><li>Alpha Blockers</li><li>Angiotensin Receptor Blockers</li><li>Renin Inhibitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hypertension drug market includes various classes of medications targeting high blood pressure. Calcium channel blockers relax blood vessels, while diuretics reduce fluid volume. Beta blockers decrease heart rate and workload. Vasodilators directly widen blood vessels. Angiotensin Converting Enzyme (ACE) inhibitors prevent the conversion of angiotensin, leading to blood vessel relaxation. Alpha blockers relax certain muscles and help improve blood flow. Angiotensin Receptor Blockers (ARBs) block the effects of angiotensin, and renin inhibitors lower renin production, reducing blood pressure.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Fiber Coupler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Couplers</li><li>Multimode Couplers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber coupler market is primarily divided into two types: single-mode couplers and multimode couplers. Single-mode couplers are designed for optical fibers that allow only one mode of light to propagate, providing high precision and low loss for long-distance transmission. In contrast, multimode couplers support multiple light modes, making them suitable for shorter distances and applications requiring higher bandwidth. Both types play crucial roles in telecommunications, networking, and data transmission, catering to distinct end-user needs.</p></p>

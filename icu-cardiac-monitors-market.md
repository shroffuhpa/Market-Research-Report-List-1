<p><strong>The ICU Cardiac Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Implantable Cardiac Monitors</li><li>Conventional Cardiac Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ICU cardiac monitors market encompasses two main types: implantable cardiac monitors and conventional cardiac monitors. Implantable cardiac monitors are small devices surgically placed beneath the skin to continuously track heart activity, offering long-term arrhythmia detection and patient monitoring. In contrast, conventional cardiac monitors are external devices used in intensive care settings to provide real-time monitoring of vital signs and cardiovascular parameters, assisting healthcare professionals in managing acute cardiac conditions effectively. Both play crucial roles in patient care.</p></p>

<p><p>The street light controllers market comprises two main types: analog and digital. Analog controllers utilize electrical components to manage lighting intensity based on preset levels, offering a straightforward and cost-effective solution. In contrast, digital controllers employ advanced technology, including sensors and microprocessors, enabling automated adjustments based on real-time data, such as ambient light and traffic conditions. This flexibility enhances energy efficiency and reduces operational costs, making digital controllers increasingly popular in smart city initiatives and modern infrastructure projects.</p></p>

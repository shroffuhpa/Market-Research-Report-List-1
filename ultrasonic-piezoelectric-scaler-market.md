<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Piezoelectric Scaler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Piezoelectric Scaler</li><li>Electric Piezo Scaler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic piezoelectric scaler market is categorized into two primary types: pneumatic piezoelectric scalers and electric piezoelectric scalers. Pneumatic scalers use compressed air to generate ultrasonic vibrations, making them lightweight and often more portable. Electric piezo scalers, on the other hand, rely on electrical power to produce ultrasonic waves, offering precision and consistency in performance. Both types are utilized in dental practices for efficient plaque removal and scaling, with each type catering to specific user preferences and practice requirements.</p></p>

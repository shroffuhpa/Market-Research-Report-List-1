<p><p>The fluorosurfactant market is categorized into four main types: amphoteric, nonionic, anionic, and cationic. Amphoteric surfactants exhibit both positive and negative charge properties, providing versatility in various applications. Nonionic surfactants, lacking any charge, are known for their stability and effectiveness across a wide range of pH levels. Anionic surfactants carry a negative charge, offering strong cleaning properties, while cationic surfactants possess a positive charge, enhancing antibacterial effects and improving surface wetting. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Extensive Green-Roof</li><li>Semi-intensive Green-Roof</li><li>Intensive Green-Roof</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The eco-friendly green roof market consists of three main types: extensive, semi-intensive, and intensive green roofs. Extensive green roofs feature shallow soil layers and require minimal maintenance, making them suitable for smaller structures. Semi-intensive green roofs combine characteristics of both extensive and intensive systems, allowing for a wider variety of plants and moderate maintenance. Intensive green roofs have deeper soil, supporting diverse vegetation, including trees, but require significant maintenance and structural support, ideal for urban parks and gardens.</p></p>

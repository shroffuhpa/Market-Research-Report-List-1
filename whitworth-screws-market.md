<p><strong>The Whitworth Screws Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>BSW</li><li>BSF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Whitworth screw market predominantly features two key types: British Standard Whitworth (BSW) and British Standard Fine (BSF). BSW screws have a coarse thread designed for general applications, offering strength and ease of use. In contrast, BSF screws have a finer thread, providing a tighter fit and improved resistance to loosening under vibration, making them suitable for precision applications. Both types adhere to the traditional Whitworth measurement system, ensuring compatibility in engineering and manufacturing sectors across various industries.</p></p>

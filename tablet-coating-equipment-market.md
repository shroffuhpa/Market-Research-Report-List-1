<p><strong>The Tablet Coating Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Coating Pan</li><li>Perforated Coating Pan</li><li>Fluidized Bed or Air Suspension Coater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tablet coating equipment market comprises several types, including standard coating pans, perforated coating pans, and fluidized bed or air suspension coaters. Standard coating pans employ gravity to coat tablets evenly with a liquid solution, while perforated coating pans enhance airflow for improved drying and coating efficiency. Fluidized bed coaters utilize a stream of air to suspend tablets, ensuring uniform coating with minimal solvent use. Each type caters to specific production needs, offering varying efficiency and coating quality.</p></p>

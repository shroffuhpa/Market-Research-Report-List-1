<p><strong>The Automobile Exhaust Catalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Three-way Catalyst (TWC)</li><li>Four-Way Catalyst (4WC)</li><li>Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC)</li><li>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) Catalyst</li><li>Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Catalyst</li><li>Ammonia Slip Catalyst (ASC)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile exhaust catalyst market encompasses several types, each serving distinct roles in emissions control. The Three-way Catalyst (TWC) reduces nitrogen oxides, carbon monoxide, and unburned hydrocarbons. The Four-Way Catalyst (4WC) enhances TWC capabilities, especially in advanced engine designs. Diesel Oxidation Catalysts (DOC) oxidize carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons. Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) catalysts convert nitrogen oxides into harmless nitrogen. Diesel Particulate Filters (DPF) capture soot, while Ammonia Slip Catalysts (ASC) mitigate excess ammonia emissions. Additional catalyst types cater to specific filtration and reduction needs.</p></p>

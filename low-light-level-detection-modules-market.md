<p><p>Low-Light-Level Detection Modules are categorized into two types: Analog Modules and Digital Modules. Analog Modules convert light signals into continuous electrical signals, providing a real-time response and high sensitivity for detecting faint light sources. Conversely, Digital Modules process light signals into discrete values, often featuring advanced signal processing and noise reduction capabilities. These digital units offer enhanced reliability and precision, making them suitable for various applications in fields like security, astronomy, and industrial monitoring, where low-light conditions are prevalent.</p></p>

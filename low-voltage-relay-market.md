<p><strong>The Low Voltage Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Voltage Relay</li><li>Static Voltage Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Voltage Relay market encompasses two primary types: Electromagnetic Voltage Relays and Static Voltage Relays. Electromagnetic Voltage Relays operate using electromagnetic coils to open or close contacts in response to voltage changes, offering reliability in various applications. In contrast, Static Voltage Relays leverage solid-state components, providing faster response times and higher durability, making them ideal for modern electronic systems. Together, these relays serve diverse sectors like industrial automation, power distribution, and renewable energy management.</p></p>

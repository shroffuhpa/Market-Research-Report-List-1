<p><p>Cloud Services Brokerage (CSB) encompasses two main market types: Internal Brokerage Enablement and External Brokerage Enablement. Internal Brokerage Enablement focuses on organizations creating and managing their own cloud service portfolio, optimizing resource use and governance within their infrastructure. In contrast, External Brokerage Enablement involves third-party brokers who provide a marketplace for various cloud services, facilitating access and integration for businesses. Both models aim to simplify cloud service management and enhance flexibility, ensuring organizations can efficiently leverage cloud resources.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Tire Pressure Monitoring Systems (TPMS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-way TPMS</li><li>Two-way TPMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tire Pressure Monitoring Systems (TPMS) enhance vehicle safety by continuously monitoring tire pressure. The market is divided into two types: One-way TPMS, which transmits data from the tire sensors to the car's control unit without receiving any feedback, and Two-way TPMS, which allows for bi-directional communication between the sensors and the vehicle, enabling real-time monitoring and alerts. Both systems aim to prevent tire-related accidents and improve fuel efficiency by ensuring optimal tire pressure.</p></p>

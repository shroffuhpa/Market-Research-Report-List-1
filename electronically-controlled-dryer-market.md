<p><p>The electronically controlled dryer market is primarily divided into two types: split type and integrated. Split type dryers consist of separate components for drying and control, allowing for flexible installation and easier maintenance. In contrast, integrated dryers combine the drying system and control mechanisms into a single unit, offering a compact design and streamlined operation. Both types cater to varying consumer needs, with split types often preferred for larger spaces and integrated models favored for convenience in smaller areas.</p></p>

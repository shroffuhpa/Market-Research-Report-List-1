<p><p>The UV resistant epoxy market is primarily segmented into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based epoxies offer robust adhesion and resilience, making them suitable for industrial applications where durability against UV exposure is crucial. They tend to cure quickly and withstand harsh conditions but may emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Conversely, water-based epoxies are eco-friendlier, with lower VOC emissions, making them ideal for residential and commercial settings. They provide good UV resistance while being easier to clean and handle.</p></p>

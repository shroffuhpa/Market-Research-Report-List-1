<p><p>Complementary DNA microarrays (cDNA) are powerful tools used in molecular biology to analyze gene expression. They consist of a glass or silicon slide onto which numerous cDNA probes are attached. These probes hybridize with complementary DNA from samples, allowing researchers to monitor the expression levels of thousands of genes simultaneously. This technology has significant applications in genomics, transcriptomics, and personalized medicine, enabling advancements in disease diagnosis, drug discovery, and biomedical research.</p><p>The cDNA microarrays market is witnessing substantial growth due to the increasing demand for personalized medicine and advancements in genomics. Innovations in microarray technology, such as higher sensitivity and specificity, are driving market expansion. Moreover, the growing prevalence of chronic diseases, coupled with a rising emphasis on early diagnosis and targeted therapies, is further fueling market demand. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.4% during the forecast period, indicating robust potential.

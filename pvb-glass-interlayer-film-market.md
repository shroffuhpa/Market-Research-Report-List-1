<p><p>The PVB glass interlayer film market is segmented into two primary types: Standard Polyvinyl Butyral (PVB) and Structural Polyvinyl Butyral. Standard PVB is widely used in automotive and architectural applications for its excellent adhesive properties, transparency, and acoustic insulation. In contrast, Structural PVB offers enhanced rigidity and strength, making it ideal for applications requiring high load-bearing capacity, such as laminated safety glass in skyscrapers and large-scale constructions. Both types contribute to safety and performance in glass laminating processes.</p></p>

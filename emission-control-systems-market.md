<p><p>The emission control systems market includes various technologies designed to reduce pollutants released into the atmosphere. Electrostatic precipitators use electric charges to capture dust and particulate matter. Absorbers remove gaseous pollutants through chemical reactions or adsorption processes. Catalytic reactors facilitate reactions that convert harmful emissions into less toxic substances. Catalytic converters, typically found in vehicles, transform harmful gases from engine exhaust into less harmful emissions using catalysts. Each system plays a crucial role in promoting cleaner air and environmental sustainability.</p></p>

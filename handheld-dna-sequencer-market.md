<p><strong>The Handheld DNA Sequencer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nanopore sequencing</li><li>Single-Molecule Real-Time Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld DNA sequencer market features primarily two types: nanopore sequencing and single-molecule real-time (SMRT) sequencing. Nanopore sequencing utilizes protein nanopores to detect DNA strands as they pass through, enabling rapid, real-time analysis. In contrast, SMRT sequencing employs zero-mode waveguides to observe the synthesis of DNA in real-time, allowing for high accuracy and long-read lengths. Both technologies enhance portability and accessibility in genomic analysis, catering to research, clinical, and field applications in various environments.</p></p>

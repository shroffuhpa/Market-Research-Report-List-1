<p><ul><li>Lamp-based</li><li>Laser-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rapid Thermal Annealer market is divided into two primary types: lamp-based and laser-based systems. Lamp-based annealers utilize high-intensity lamps to quickly heat semiconductor wafers, ensuring uniform temperature distribution and efficient thermal processing. In contrast, laser-based annealers employ focused laser beams for precise heating, allowing for targeted treatment of specific areas on a substrate. Both technologies are crucial in semiconductor manufacturing for enhancing material properties, improving device performance, and facilitating advanced fabrication techniques.</p></p>

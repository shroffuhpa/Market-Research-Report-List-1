<p><p>The mill liner market is primarily divided into two types: metal mill liners and rubber mill liners. Metal mill liners, often made from materials like steel or alloys, are known for their durability and wear resistance, making them suitable for high-impact and heavy-duty applications. In contrast, rubber mill liners offer better noise reduction and are lighter, providing flexibility in installation and maintenance. Each type serves distinct operational needs, impacting performance, cost, and longevity in various milling processes.</p></p>

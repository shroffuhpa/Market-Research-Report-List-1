<p><ul><li>Nd-YAG Laser</li><li>Photodynamic Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pulmonology lasers market primarily features two types: Nd:YAG lasers and photodynamic lasers. Nd:YAG lasers are utilized for therapeutic procedures, effectively treating airway obstructions and lung tumors by delivering precise energy to target tissues. Photodynamic lasers, on the other hand, work in conjunction with photosensitizing agents, activating them to destroy malignant cells upon exposure to specific light wavelengths. Both types enhance minimally invasive techniques in lung therapies, improving patient outcomes and expanding treatment options within pulmonology.</p></p>

<p><p>Keylock switches are secure devices that use a key to operate electrical circuits. Single-pole keylock switches control one circuit and are commonly used in low-voltage applications like machinery or lighting. They offer straightforward functionality and are ideal for basic on/off operations. In contrast, double-pole keylock switches manage two circuits simultaneously, providing enhanced control for higher voltage applications and more complex systems. This allows for greater flexibility in electrical control setups, making them suitable for industrial settings.</p></p>

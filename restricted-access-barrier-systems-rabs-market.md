<p><p>Restricted Access Barrier Systems (RABS) are designed to enhance the aseptic environment in pharmaceutical manufacturing. They minimize operator intervention while providing controlled access to sterile areas. The market is divided into passive RABS, which rely on physical barriers and maintain positive pressure without active airflow, and active RABS, which incorporate dynamic airflow systems to further reduce contamination risk. Both types aim to ensure product integrity and compliance with stringent regulatory standards in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>

<p><p>The coconut-derived emollients market includes three main types: humectants, occlusives, and lubricants. Humectants attract moisture to the skin, enhancing hydration and preventing dryness. Occlusives form a protective barrier on the skin's surface, sealing in moisture and providing a smooth feel. Lubricants improve the texture and spreadability of products, offering a silky touch and facilitating easier application. These categories cater to diverse cosmetic and personal care applications, leveraging the natural properties of coconut to enhance skin health and product performance.</p></p>

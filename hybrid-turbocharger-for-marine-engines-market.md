<p><p>The hybrid turbocharger market for marine engines comprises three main types: single turbo, twin turbo, and variable geometry turbo. Single turbo systems are efficient and compact, ideal for smaller vessels. Twin turbo setups enhance performance by providing power across a broader range of RPMs, improving responsiveness. Variable geometry turbochargers adjust their blade angles, optimizing airflow for varying engine speeds, which enhances efficiency and reduces emissions. Together, these types enable better performance, fuel efficiency, and adaptability in marine applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The Generator Circuit Breaker (GCB) market consists of various types, including Vacuum Circuit Breakers and SF6 Circuit Breakers. Vacuum Circuit Breakers utilize a sealed chamber to extinguish arcs, making them efficient and reliable for medium voltage applications. SF6 Circuit Breakers use sulfur hexafluoride gas for insulation and arc extinction, offering superior performance in high voltage settings. Other types may include oil-insulated and air-insulated breakers, each with distinct benefits catering to specific operational needs in power generation and distribution systems.</p></p>

<p><p>Edge server market types include Blade, Rack, HCI (Hyper-Converged Infrastructure), and other formats. Blade servers are compact, modular units that optimize space and power, making them ideal for data centers. Rack servers are standalone units that fit into standardized racks, providing scalability and efficient management. HCI integrates storage, computing, and networking into a single system for simplified deployment and management. Other market types may encompass specialized appliances or integrated systems tailored for specific edge computing applications, enhancing flexibility and performance.</p></p>

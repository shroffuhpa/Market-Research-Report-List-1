<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Self-healing Concrete Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrinsic Healing</li><li>Capsule Based Healing</li><li>Vascular Healing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-healing concrete market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Intrinsic Healing** utilizes the natural properties of concrete materials to repair small cracks over time through hydration and chemical processes.</p><p>2. **Capsule-Based Healing** involves embedding microcapsules filled with healing agents in the concrete mix, which break upon cracking to release the material that fills and seals the damage.</p><p>3. **Vascular Healing** incorporates a network of channels within the concrete, allowing a healing agent to flow and fill cracks when they occur, ensuring enhanced durability and longevity.</p></p>

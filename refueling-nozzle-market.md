<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refueling nozzle market is divided into two primary types: manual and automatic nozzles. Manual refueling nozzles require the operator to control the flow of fuel, offering flexibility in various fueling applications but potentially leading to spills or overfilling. In contrast, automatic nozzles feature mechanisms that detect when the tank is full, ceasing fuel flow independently. This enhances safety and efficiency by minimizing human error. Both types cater to different user preferences and operational requirements in fuel dispensing.</p></p>

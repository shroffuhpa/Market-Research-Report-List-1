<p><p>The probe rocket market comprises two main types: one-stage rockets and multi-stage rockets. One-stage rockets utilize a single propulsion system to reach their destination, making them simpler and often more cost-effective for short missions. In contrast, multi-stage rockets employ multiple stages that detach during flight, allowing for greater payload capacity and efficiency in reaching higher altitudes or speeds. Each type serves distinct mission profiles, with one-stage rockets suitable for straightforward tasks and multi-stage rockets ideal for complex space exploration endeavors.</p></p>
